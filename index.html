Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?

I offer UI/UX design, frontend development, branding, and consultation services. Each project is approached with a focus on user experience, visual appeal, and business goals.

What is your design process?

My design process typically involves discovery (understanding your needs), research, wireframing, prototyping, design, testing, and implementation. I believe in an iterative approach with continuous feedback.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity. A simple website might take 2-3 weeks, while a complex application could take 2-3 months. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial discussion.
